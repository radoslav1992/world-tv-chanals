## Overview

La 1 is the flagship television channel of RTVE, Spain's national public broadcaster. It launched on 28 October 1956 as the first television service in Spain and remains the corporation's principal general-interest channel.

## History

Television Española held a monopoly on Spanish television for decades and played an important role in the country's social and cultural life, including during its transition to democracy in the 1970s. La 1 has continued as the public broadcaster's main channel through the arrival of private competitors and the shift to digital terrestrial television.

## Programming

La 1 carries a broad schedule of news, primetime drama and series, entertainment and variety formats, and live coverage of major sporting and national events. Its news service, *Telediario*, is among the most watched in Spain, and the channel traditionally broadcasts significant state and cultural occasions.

## Significance

As the lead channel of Spain's public broadcaster, La 1 holds a central place in Spanish television. Its general-interest programming and national event coverage make it a key part of the country's media landscape.
