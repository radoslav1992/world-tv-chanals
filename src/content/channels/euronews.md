## Overview

Euronews is a pan-European television news channel that launched on 1 January 1993. Headquartered in Lyon, France, it is distinctive for broadcasting simultaneously in many languages, allowing viewers across Europe and beyond to watch the same news in their own tongue.

## History

Euronews was founded by a consortium of European public broadcasters to provide a shared European perspective on news, in contrast to the predominantly Anglo-American international channels. Ownership has changed over the years, but its multilingual model has remained central to its identity.

## Programming

The channel offers rolling news, weather, business and culture coverage, and analysis of European affairs. A signature feature is its multilingual audio, with the same footage narrated in numerous languages. Euronews places particular emphasis on the workings of the European Union and on stories that span national borders.

## Significance

Euronews occupies a unique position as a genuinely pan-European broadcaster. Its multilingual approach and focus on continental affairs make it a notable alternative to national and Anglo-American news channels.
