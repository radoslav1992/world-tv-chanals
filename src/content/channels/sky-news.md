## Overview

Sky News is a British free-to-air rolling news channel operated by Sky Group, part of Comcast. Launched on 5 February 1989, it was the United Kingdom's first 24-hour television news service, predating the BBC's own continuous news channel by several years.

## History

Sky News was a trailblazer for round-the-clock news in Britain and built a reputation for speed and breaking-news coverage. It has won numerous broadcasting awards over the years and expanded its reach across satellite, cable, digital terrestrial television and online streaming.

## Programming

The channel's output is built around rolling bulletins, live event coverage, business and political reporting, and signature interview and debate programmes. Sky News is known for its fast-paced breaking-news operation and its coverage of UK politics, including general-election nights and major national events.

## Significance

As the UK's first 24-hour news channel, Sky News helped shape the modern news landscape in Britain. It remains a major competitor to the BBC and a widely watched source of national and international news.
