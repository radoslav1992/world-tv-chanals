Europe is home to some of the most respected news broadcasters in the world, and many of them stream live online for free. Whether you want a British, French, German or pan-European perspective on world events, there is a channel for you. Here is a guide to the leading European news channels you can [watch in your browser](/) today.

## BBC News

The BBC's rolling news channel is backed by one of the largest newsgathering operations on the planet, with correspondents on every continent. In 2023 the BBC combined its domestic and international news channels into a single global service, so viewers around the world now see the same trusted bulletins, live coverage and analysis. If you want comprehensive, impartial coverage of both UK and international affairs, BBC News is the natural starting point.

## Sky News

Sky News was the United Kingdom's first 24-hour news channel, launching in 1989 — years ahead of the BBC's own continuous news service. It built its reputation on speed and breaking news, and remains a fast-paced alternative for UK politics and international stories. It is free-to-air in Britain and widely available online.

## France 24

For a French and European perspective, France 24 is hard to beat. Launched in 2006 and operated by the public group France Médias Monde, it broadcasts in French, English, Arabic and Spanish. Its free online and YouTube streams make it one of the most accessible international channels, and its multilingual model gives it a genuinely global outlook.

## DW (Deutsche Welle)

DW is Germany's international public broadcaster, offering news, business reporting and a strong line-up of documentaries in English. Funded from the German federal budget, it aims to provide independent information worldwide. Its explainer journalism and documentary strands have built a large following on streaming platforms.

## Euronews

Euronews is the most distinctly European of the bunch. Launched in 1993 and based in Lyon, it broadcasts the same footage in many languages at once, so viewers across the continent can watch in their own tongue. It places particular emphasis on the European Union and on cross-border stories that national channels sometimes overlook.

## Which one should you choose?

Each channel brings a different angle:

- **BBC News** — the gold standard for breadth and impartiality.
- **Sky News** — fast breaking news with a UK focus.
- **France 24** — a French and Francophone perspective, in multiple languages.
- **DW** — German viewpoint, excellent documentaries.
- **Euronews** — a pan-European outlook in your own language.
